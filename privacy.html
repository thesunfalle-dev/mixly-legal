2. Scope of this policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Mixly handles information when you use the Mixly mobile application and related hosted legal or catalog services. It is designed to describe the current first-release behavior of the app.

3. Information stored on your device

Mixly stores some app data locally on your device so the app can work without an account. This may include:

  • onboarding preferences, such as selected brands, categories, flavors, and preferred strength;
  • favorite mix identifiers;
  • local app state needed to show your personalized mix feed;
  • language-related app behavior derived from your device locale.

This information is stored on your device using local storage technology such as AsyncStorage. In the current release, these preferences and favorites are not stored in a Mixly user account and are not synced to a Mixly user profile.

4. Information processed by backend and service providers

Mixly uses Supabase as a service provider to host read-only catalog data, such as brands, flavors, mix compositions, source references, and related metadata. The app requests this catalog data from Supabase so it can display and rank mixes.

When your device connects to Supabase or to hosting services used for legal pages or app infrastructure, those providers may process technical information needed to operate, secure, and troubleshoot the service. This may include IP address, request metadata, device or network metadata, timestamps, error logs, and security logs.

We use this technical information only for service functionality, security, debugging, abuse prevention, and legal or operational compliance. We do not use it for advertising tracking.

5. Feedback and suggestion messages

The app lets you send suggestions, catalog requests, or feedback from inside Mixly. If you submit a message, Mixly sends the message text, message type, app language, source label, timestamp, and related technical request data to our Supabase Edge Function.

That function forwards the message to a Telegram chat used by the Mixly operator for support and product operations. Telegram may process the message content and related metadata under its own terms and privacy practices.

We use feedback and suggestion messages only to respond where possible, provide support, improve the app, improve the catalog, investigate issues, and keep necessary operational or legal records. We do not use these messages for advertising or marketing unless we separately ask for and receive any legally required consent.

Please do not include sensitive personal information in feedback or suggestion messages unless it is necessary for your request.

6. Data we do not collect in the current release

In the current release, Mixly does not provide or use:

  • account registration or login;
  • in-app purchases, subscriptions, or payment processing;
  • advertising SDKs or ad personalization;
  • analytics SDKs;
  • public user-generated content, public profiles, comments, chats, or posts;
  • camera, photo library, contacts, precise location, coarse location, microphone, or push notification permissions.
